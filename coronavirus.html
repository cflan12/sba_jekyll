As the world confronts COVID-19, we hope for your safety and wellbeing. Coronavirus (COVID-19) is impacting our country in an unprecedented way. We are proud members of the San Diego community and understand that each of you is going through unique challenges.

South Bay Autohaus will remain open as conditions permit by State and Federal authorities to provide necessary service for our customers.

Our first and foremost priority is the safety of our customers and employees.

We respectfully ask our customers to observe social distancing.

We would like to decrease the need for individuals to physically wait for their vehicles at South Bay Autohaus to avoid interaction between large groups of people.
